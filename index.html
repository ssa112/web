Perlite

We sold and delivered this perlite to one of our clients who operates strawberry farm in Indonesia. This versatile mineral boasts properties that enhance plant drainage, making it particularly advantageous for crops like strawberries, which thrive in oxygen-rich environments. Given the high oxygen demand of strawberry roots, substrates with excellent porosity are essential, and perlite fits the bill perfectly. Moreover, perlite finds widespread application beyond agriculture, serving as a key component in hydroponic plant cultivation and offering insulation solutions for both buildings and tanks. For further details or inquiries, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. We are here to provide comprehensive information and assistance tailored to your specific needs.

Sedicell System Project

PT SSA with ACE Pressureweld Ltd has successfully installed the new sedicell system project in PT Gayabaru Paperindo, Indonesia. The installation for PM1 took place in 2018, followed by PM2 in 2022, and both systems have been operating efficiently ever since. The paper mill has achieved notable improvements, including enhanced water treatment efficiency, consistently maintaining total suspended solids (TSS) levels below 100ppm, and a higher percentage of fiber recovery. ACE's Sedicell system has demonstrated its capability to achieve zero discharge of wastewater and enhance fiber recovery efficiency. This innovative solution guarantees a reduction of over 95% in TSS and has proven to be effective under varying TSS input conditions, ranging from low to extreme levels (up to 10,000ppm). We invite you to consult with us regarding your current requirements, and together, we can devise a tailored solution to address your specific needs.

Cutting Edge Pressure Cylinder Former

PT SSA in collaboration with ACE Pressureweld we have effectively engineered and implemented a cutting-edge pressure cylinder former for one of our clients. This client is manufacturing low gsm specialty paper and plan to enhance the machine speed. In response, we proposed the adoption of a pressure former, highlighting its numerous advantages: enhanced sheet strength and formation, precise MD/CD sheet control, increased speed capabilities, and simplified operation and maintenance procedures. following the installation of the pressure cylinder former (complete with the octopus headbox) from ACE Pressureweld, the outcome has been exceedingly positive, with the client expressing satisfaction and experiencing a remarkable increase in machine speed of over 50%, achieved better formation and sheet control and more.
